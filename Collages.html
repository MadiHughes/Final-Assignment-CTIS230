Collages

Collages are forms of art made by gluing, taping, or otherwise attaching or placing various different materials such as photographs, newspaper clippings, etc. Here are three examples of physical newspaper collages, showcasing various shades of red, orange, and violet. Cut by hand with an exacto knife, these collage elements are individually placed within each black square and/or rectangle. Despite the fact that they may have no particular narrative meaning, the formation of full pieces by placing unrelated pieces together to make a full piece is very interesting, and is a very intricate process.
