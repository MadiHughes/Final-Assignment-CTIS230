Fine Art

Fine art relates to the depiction of what many view as "normal" art- such as painting and scuplture from the Renaissance period. Physical paintings, like the ones shown below, often used reds, oranges, and violets to symbolize certain themes or values. Red, for example, usually represented war, love, or power, while violets were usually used to represent wealth, royalty, and ambition.

"Napoleon am St.Bernhard" painted by Jacques-Louis David (left). "Botanical Self-Portrait" by Madison Hughes (right)
