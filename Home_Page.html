The Many Different Kinds of Art

An Exploration into the Red Color Family

In the world of art, there exists a pleathora of colors. Each of these colors comes in various shades, hues, and tones that create the wonderful visual spectrum. In particular, the color red, along with its analogous compliments- orange and violet, along with their many shades, make up what is know as one of several color families.

The Red Color Family consists of every shade, hue, and tone of red, red-orange, orange, orange-violet, and violet. Though not every single color is shown in the above images, they do provide a glimpse into the spectrum that this being deferred to throughout this website.
